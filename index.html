About Me

From creating wellness programs to developing instructional products, the one true thing that describes me well is... I'M A CREATOR.

I'm the Founder and Owner of Primal Platoon, Inc. However, this year I've decided to slow down business operations to become a software developer.

I'm currently attending a coding bootcamp, and freelancing as an instructional designer and corporate trainer.

My goal is to break into tech to become a full stack developer. I plan to integrate my experience in wellness and adult learning with the technology (and software products) that will help people live happier, healthier lives.

I live in Marlborough, MA with my wife, Daisy, and two children. I enjoy traveling to other countries with my family, and yes, trying out local craft beers in every country I visit. I happen to be a US Military Veteran, but I'll save my war stories for another day.
